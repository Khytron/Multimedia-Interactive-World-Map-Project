About This Project

The Interactive World Map: Culture & History is an educational tool designed to foster spatial-historical literacy across five key regions:

  • Asia - Explore the Silk Road and ancient dynasties
  • Middle East - Discover the Islamic Golden Age
  • Europe - Journey through the Renaissance
  • Africa - Learn about pre-colonial empires
  • Australia - Understand Indigenous history

Use the timeline slider to travel through time from 3000 BCE to the present day. Click on cultural markers to explore detailed information about historical events, figures, and traditions.
